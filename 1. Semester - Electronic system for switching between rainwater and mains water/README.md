# Automated Rainwater Switching System | Sep. 2021 – Dec. 2021
This project sought to investigate the possibility of utilising rainwater in private gardens more efficiently to help people lower their consumption of mains water and contribute to the UN’s Sustainable Development Goal number 12 concerning Responsible Consumption and Production. To achieve this, a prototype has been created which includes an electronic system made of both hardware and software. The system and its prototype are able to automatically switch between using rainwater and water from the mains supply in existing irrigation systems, based on the availability of rainwater.
