# Controlled Environment for Plant Growth and Monitoring | Feb. 2022 – July 2022
In this project, the practice of vertical farming and the advantages and disadvantages of vertical farming compared to conventional farming are explored. The basics of farming are also explored, what plants need in order to grow and how these affect their growth. Vertical farming will also be looked at through the lens of the UN Sustainable Development Goals, and whether or not it can help achieve these and if so, in what manner. This culminates in making a test chamber with a controllable environment, and the testing thereof.
